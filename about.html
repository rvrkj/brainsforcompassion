About us

Brains for Compassion (BforC) has been successfully working as a catalyst for change and positive impact since March 2020. Our mission is to introduce the best practices to strengthen the positive impact around the world, simultaneously creating an equal space for both men and women, and to grow together.

Meet Varsha Kothari (Founder, Brains for Compassion)

We human beings have options to select and choose what we like to be and where we see ourselves in future. I have gone through this phase in my life and I am very proud to say that I choose to work for the communities around us which fulfil my soul to the best. Since childhood, I have learned and gained aspirations and inspirations from the people around me. I always wanted to support women and girls so that they can have their voice and that to be heard. The social dilemma in which we are living is biased for women in so many ways regardless of how much progress we are making. Through Brains for Compassion, wings of hope will be nurtured so that we all can fly with our dreams and desires.

Brains for Compassion (BforC) came into existence with the same inspiration and motivation. Women have all the power to transform the society, the only thing needed is to support and embrace their knowledge and wisdom. We, BfoC came with an idea to start an all women organisation where womens from different parts of the world can come together and help each other to grow in whatever possible manner they can.

Let's be part of this journey and bring out the best among all!
