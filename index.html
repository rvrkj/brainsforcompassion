Brains for Compassion (BforC) is a fully women-led organisation working as a strong catalyst for change, by partnering up with various organisations and institutions, striving to bring a positive impact within communities. Women from different parts of the world have joined hands and are working with BforC to bring their knowledge and wisdom together at one roof and to "provide global solutions to local problems"...
